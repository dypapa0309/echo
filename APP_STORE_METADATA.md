## Full Description Draft

Echo is a voice-first memo app built for fast capture.

Record a thought, meeting note, or to-do as soon as it happens. Echo keeps the flow simple: record, review, and come back to what matters.

With Echo you can:

- record voice memos quickly
- revisit saved notes in a clean archive
- review a short summary and original note together
- group related memos by topic
- mark important memos to follow up later
- keep your notes on-device for a lighter, more private workflow

Echo is designed for people who want a fast memo habit without a heavy setup. Open the app, record what matters, and return to it when you need it.

## App Review Notes Draft

Echo is an on-device voice memo app.

Core flow:

1. Record a memo
2. Save it locally
3. Review summary, original note, and grouped topics

Permissions:

- Microphone: required to record voice memos
- Speech recognition: required to convert speech into text when supported
- Calendar: optional, used only to connect a memo to the current schedule context

Billing:

- If billing is enabled for submission, the app includes Restore Purchases in Settings

Wake-word note:

- "Hey Echo" is designed for foreground or app-resume usage and is not presented as unrestricted full background wake-word behavior
