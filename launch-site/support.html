Fast help for recording, memo, billing, and permission issues.

Contact

Email: hello@example.com

Recommended issue report format

  • Device model
  • iOS or Android version
  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • Whether microphone, speech, and calendar permissions were allowed

Common checks

  • Make sure microphone permission is enabled.
  • Make sure speech recognition permission is enabled if transcription is expected.
  • For calendar-linked memos, confirm calendar permission is enabled.
  • Use Restore Purchases in Settings if a paid entitlement is missing.
